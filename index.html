ABOUT US

The Indian Premier League (IPL) (also known as the TATA IPL for sponsorship reasons) is a men's Twenty20 (T20) cricket league that is annually held in India and contested by ten city-based franchise teams. The BCCI founded the league in 2007.

AUCTION

The IPL's all eight franchises compete in an annual auction to secure domestic, national and International players with a limited budget.
